Solar pays for the roof

Solar can produce electricity at a lower cost than your utility company. The more you produce and use your own electricity, the less you purchase. Over time, your solar energy may even pay for itself AND your new roof, via monthly electric bill savings and government incentives.

You may also get credit for solar energy you send back to the grid when your panels produce more power than your home uses. This could further increase your monthly savings.

Of course, savings will vary and are not guaranteed. Incentives and eligibility requirements vary and availability is not guaranteed.
